This dataset was designed to validate slice order of mosaic images acquired with different slice orientation on a  Siemens B17. The participant violently rotates the head during one of the volumes, revealing the slice order. For details see
	http://www.mccauslandcenter.sc.edu/crnl/tools/stc
In addition, the images jpg1.dcm and jpg2.dcm were converted to the lossless-JPEG format unique to DICOM (Transfer Syntax 1.2.840.10008.1.2.4.70) using dcmcjpeg version 3.6. The files jp2k1.dcm and jp2k2.dcm were converted to lossless-JPEG2000 (1.2.840.10008.1.2.4.90) using Osirix.
